select-disabled

Disables the interactivity of options if the expression is evaluated to be true. The evaluation has the access to the additional local scope with $optIndex, $optValue, $optSelected varaibles to increase the usage flexibility.

This optional directive is applicable to both ng-select and ng-select-option, each stands for global and option specific configuration.

In the example below, the third and fifth options are always disabled.
